Online Guitar Archive

This is a side project I have been designing and planning. I want somewhere people can go to get exact information, specs, how to, and upgrades for any guitar by year and brand. Search by Brand to narrow down to every make and model in history. With options to view information, videos, upgrades, etc all in one spot. The amount of forum, video, and word of mouth digging needed to get good information is so scattered, lets archive it!
