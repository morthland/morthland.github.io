UH72 Helicopter Trainer

Disti and Design Interactive co-developed a training center for the Navy and its UH72 Helicopter division. Teaching soldiers the ins and outs of repairing a UH-72 Helicopter. I helped develop and design a training system for an instructor to oversee 12 workstations. Each workstations is comprised of a 55 inch touch screen monitor and two auxiliary touch displays below.

Working closely with Subject Matter Experts and Naval Instructor recommendations. The entire system is operated via touch, so all the UX and design had to work under various conditions. There are many windows and procedures that can happen at once, across 3 screens, keeping end user focus was key in these scenarios.

Managing and keeping things contained and compartmentalized was also paramount in keeping the ease of use simple. Various types of people will be using the system and barrier of entry has to be low. Design Interactive's contract was for the interface development and user experience only. Finalized graphics and software development were completed by Disti.
