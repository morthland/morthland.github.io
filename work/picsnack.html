picSnack

A search engine for social photography with tasty little goodies.

View and create galleries with your Instagram feed. picSnack was a web app that aggregates images based upon hashtag and creates custom galleries to share and embed.

I was hired to create the user experience, user interface, and graphic design for picSnack.
Sadly, picSnack was overrun with competitors at launch in 2012 and has closed down.

Client: Real Focus Media
