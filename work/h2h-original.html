Hero 2 Hired

The Department of Defense and Yellow Ribbon program commissioned for a government funded military occupation job site. H2H.jobs offers various ways for veterans to search and find jobs based on their service or MOS.

This campaign contained dozens of components including websites, mobile apps, posters, banners, vehicle wraps, emails, kiosk booths and many others. I worked on each and every component from start to finish with the help of the art director.

H2H.jobs (archived site):
Main website for the Department of Defense and Yellow Ribbon campaign.

H2H App:
Everything you need to find a job. Take a Career Assessment or browse jobs based on MOS. Stay up to date on news and social items.

H2H Career Assistant App:
Everything you need to find a job. Integrated dashboard, messaging, scheduler, and many other components to help veterans on the job hunt.

H2H Media:
Video of the H2H program and results.
