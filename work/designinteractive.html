Design Interactive

Responsive website redesign for Design Interactive, Inc.

Design Interactive offers Research and Development services to Government and Consumer markets. As the UX Designer my first personal goal at DI was to completely rebuild the neglected wordpress site into a modern day winner. My goal was to increase the user experience throughout the website and provide better visibility into products and services. I spearheaded the entire process from conception to design to coding. With some help from management on business copy.

The old wordpress website was ancient. Slow loading, large file sizes, not mobile friendly. Not to mention the scary navigation and information architecture. Redesigned using the latest and greatest bootstrap framework and CSS3 techniques, the site is now responsive and up with the times. With an easy to navigate design and proper design architecture, the site is future proof for years to come.
